About Us

UNDRAFTED was born from a love for basketball and hip-hop. We are a brand that celebrates both cultures, blending them into unique clothing that speaks to those who are passionate about both worlds. Our aim is to create something that feels like home, something that speaks to your roots and your journey.

Our Story

UNDRAFTED was created by individuals who grew up with basketball courts as their second home and speakers blasting hip-hop as their soundtrack. We wanted to create more than just a brand – we wanted to build a community. A place where individuals who share our love for both basketball and hip-hop can express their identity, their journey, and their culture.

Each piece of clothing we design represents our roots, our hustle, and our dedication to what we love. UNDRAFTED is not just a clothing brand – it’s a lifestyle, a movement that celebrates staying true to oneself, whether on the court or in the streets.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the boundaries between music, sports, and fashion are blurred, and everyone feels connected through what they wear. UNDRAFTED is here to help you express your passion for the game and the culture, while looking great and feeling confident. Our vision is to empower the next generation of creatives, athletes, and dreamers who make their own paths, no matter the challenges they face.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals through fashion that reflects the heart of the streets, the rhythm of hip-hop, and the spirit of the game. We aim to provide high-quality, meaningful apparel that fuels confidence, pride, and self-expression. UNDRAFTED is here to remind you that even if you weren’t chosen — you can still choose yourself.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We believe in staying true to our roots and our culture.
  • Creativity: Innovation and unique designs are at the heart of everything we do.
  • Community: We aim to bring people together through shared passion and mutual respect.
  • Quality: We are committed to delivering high-quality products that last.
  • Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and celebrate every individual’s unique journey.

The UNDRAFTED Lifestyle

At UNDRAFTED, we believe in the hustle – the journey that gets you from where you are to where you want to be. It’s about the drive, the passion, and the commitment to never settle for less. Whether you're on the court, in the studio, or on the streets, UNDRAFTED is with you every step of the way, giving you the confidence to stay true to yourself and your journey.

Join The Movement

UNDRAFTED is more than just a brand – it’s a family. It’s a community of like-minded individuals who are all about pushing boundaries and staying true to their roots. Join us and become part of the movement. Together, we’ll break through the barriers and show the world what it means to stay UNDRAFTED.
